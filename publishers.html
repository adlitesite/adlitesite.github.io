More engagement. More value.

Content based advertising is designed to drive more engagement, a precious commodity that commands premium rates from advertisers. Also, the direct link with advertisers allows for operational cost savings that are efficiently passed on to both parties.

Tailored to fit editorial content. Ad tech like never before.

We create content rich native ads from branded content. Our NLP engine matches the most relevant for each of your content page, one by one. You have full visibility and control over the process and the format. As a result, your users will see branded content that you find relevant to editorial content. High quality content promotes trust between publishers and readers while engaging them. No blind ad networks, no interruptive ads.

Fast. Lightning fast.

Adlite was designed with performance in mind. Our algorithms profile content as it's produced: branded content relevant to each article is ready before users start to browse. Adlite moves from cookie based advertising to content aware advertising - in other words, ads are specific to a page and not a user. This means no RTB and no other layers that slow down your content.
