For advertisers

Adlite is about content-based ads. It is a new way to promote branded content across multiple publishers, merging the quality of sponsored content with the reach of display advertising.

Unparalleled performance. Unprecedented level.

You write killer content that people care about. Adlite's NLP engine profiles it and finds relevant articles among top publishers’ inventory. We promote your content using Adlite’s native ad feed on publishers’ sites - directly and at scale. Delivering performance that only sponsored content can reach.

Open and transparent. See for yourself.

We profile millions of publishers' pages in real time using our NLP technology. You can use semantic matches, broad keyword searches or your own categories to actively choose your campaign’s placements and bids - even with page by page granularity for the highest brand protection. You can also dynamically match your criteria to make sure your campaign has access to articles hot off the press.

In the right place at the right time. Making the most of every opportunity.

Thanks to direct placement, you can finally track your campaign performance with absolute accuracy and have immediate feedback. But there’s much more: using this clean historical data and external sources, our predictive algorithms can help you optimise placement, timing and messaging.
